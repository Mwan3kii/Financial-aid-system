Process

How EFAMS Works

A transparent, four-step journey from application to disbursement — every stage tracked in real time.

1
Student Applies

Foreign student fills in personal, academic and financial details, uploads supporting documents, and submits the application online.

2
Embassy Verifies

An embassy verification officer reviews all documents, approves or requests corrections, then forwards verified applications to the aid provider.

3
Provider Assesses

The financial aid provider scores the application on academic, financial and need criteria, then issues an approval, partial approval, or rejection.

4
Funds Disbursed

The student receives an in-app notification of the decision with the approved amount. Funds are disbursed directly to the student's account.

Features

Everything You Need,
In One Place

Digital Application Form

Structured form capturing personal details, institution info, loan requirements and supporting documentation — all in one submission.

Real-time Status Tracking

Students see exactly where their application stands — from submission through embassy review, assessment, and final decision.

Secure Document Upload

Encrypted file storage for passports, transcripts, admission letters and financial statements with format and authenticity validation.

Multi-role Access Control

Separate dashboards and permissions for students, embassy officers, financial aid providers and system administrators.

In-app Notifications

Instant alerts for document requests, status changes, comments from officers and providers, and final decision outcomes.

Assessment Scoring Engine

Weighted scoring across academic performance, financial need and eligibility criteria, generating objective, auditable decisions.
